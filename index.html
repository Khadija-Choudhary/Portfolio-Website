About Me

My Journey

A driven computer science student with a passion for turning complex problems into elegant solutions.

My academic journey began at Dar-e-Arqam Schools, Sargodha, where I completed my matriculation. After that, I pursued my FSc in Pre-Engineering from the Punjab Group of Colleges.

Following my intermediate studies, I took a gap year to focus on developing practical skills. During this time, I completed courses in web designing and web development. Alongside this, I also learned Canva and participated in a poster-making competition, which enhanced my creativity and design skills.

Later, I enrolled in the BS Computer Science program at the University of Sargodha. From the very first semester, I gained valuable knowledge and had many new learning experiences that contributed to both my academic and personal growth.

In my second semester, I started my first internship, which was a two-month virtual internship. During this period, I learned a great deal and gained hands-on experience, making it a highly valuable and rewarding opportunity in my journey.
